Step 2

This step will show you how to mark up your dataset and apply transformations. First you will need to put your images into 2 folders to upload to roboflow. To upload you will go to the project that you have created and click upload data this will allow you to select the folders with the images to mark up. I recommend doing this one at a time as to not mix up the images during the process of marking up the images. How well you mark up the images will be shown in the accuracy of the model. Once uploaded you will click annotation for the sake of this project you will be manually annotating but this would generally be done by a team of individuals. Now click manual annotation and click start annotation. Now you will create a box around the dog or cat whichever you are doing and label it as such in the text box. Then repeat this step for all dog images and cat images. After you are done you will now go to step 3.
